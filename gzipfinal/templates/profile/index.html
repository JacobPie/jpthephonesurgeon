About Us

The story of JP the Phone Surgeon starts with a teenager who couldn't sit still. He loved to learn and grow his knowledge, constantly finding a new fascination to study. One day, he discovered a fascination with fixing phones. He loved technology and wanted to know more about what was in them and how they ran. From this fascination, spurred on by the repair of his father's broken phone, he decided to make a business. He collected all the broken devices he could and dissected them, learning every odd and end about them. In this painstaking process, he felt confident enough to fix them for people. So he started JP the Phone Surgeon, an almost completely word of mouth company. He undercut the prices of his competitors, increased the service, and made it far easier to get your device repaired than any other alternative. JP the Phone Surgeon was started in 2019 by Jacob Pieczynski, a fifteen year old who loved to learn.
